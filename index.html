Mercury

Mercury's surface temperatures are extremely hot and cold at the same time. Because the planet is so close to the Sun, day temperatures can reach 430°C but at night, temperatures can go as low as -180°C due to lack of atmosphere.Despite its proximity to the Sun, Mercury is not the hottest planet in our solar system.

But Mercury is the winner in different competitions, it's the fastest planet, zipping around the Sun every 88 Earth days, at almost 47 kilometers per second. Maybe it doesn't have its own satellite but Mercury's surface resembles that of Earth's Moon.
